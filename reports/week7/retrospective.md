# Week 7 Retrospective

## What went well
- The team efficiently tackled the final technical blockers (CORS, SPA routing, performance) raised after Week 6.
- The per-service configuration and deployment guidelines were completed comprehensively.
- We successfully integrated `xlsxwriter` without disrupting the existing chat and download logic, providing an excellent UI improvement (rich download animations).
- `MVP v3` was stabilized and tagged successfully without major regressions.
- Proactively pruning the project scope by deprecating low-priority or overly complex features (PBI-031, PBI-035, PBI-039) kept the final handover version clean and highly focused.

## What went wrong
- We were unable to schedule a meeting with the customer before the end of the sprint, which leaves the final transition and acceptance state pending.
- Handling merge conflicts at the end of the sprint (due to multiple simultaneous documentation and UI fixes) caused some temporary delays.

## What we will do differently next time
- In future projects, we should finalize meeting schedules earlier in the week to avoid missing customer touchpoints at the end of critical transition sprints.
- We will be more strict about branch isolation and merging strategies as we approach final release milestones.
