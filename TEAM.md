## 👥 Team & Contributions

The NLEx project was developed by a team of Innopolis University students. Below is a detailed list of the roles and contributions of each participant, compiled for the project portfolio.

### 🌟 Maksim Merkushev — Product Owner & Deployment Engineer

* Role: Product Owner, Deployment & Network Infrastructure Engineer.
* Contributions:
* Product backlog management, task prioritization, and coordination of sprint goals.
* Excel export optimization: replaced slow libraries with a custom CSV-to-Excel conversion mechanism, enabling the export of 120,000 rows in ~13 seconds.
* Production network infrastructure setup, resolving Nginx SPA routing issues, configuring dynamic CORS headers and Uvicorn configurations.
* LLM workflow integration and optimization, developing a RAG system for database schema filtering to save context tokens.



### 🌟 Serafim Soldatov — Scrum Master & Lead Business Analyst

* Role: Scrum Master, Lead Business Analyst, and Documentation Manager.
* Contributions:
* Organizing Scrum processes, facilitating sprint planning sessions, retrospectives, and preparing sprint reports.
* Developing product transition documentation (Customer Handover), defining the Definition of Done (DoD), and preparing final presentation materials.
* Communication and interaction with the customer, and assistance in determining the direction of product development.



### 🌟 Maksim Maltsev — Lead Developer & CI/CD Engineer

* Role: Lead Full-Stack Developer, CI/CD and DevOps Engineer.
* Contributions:

* Writing service-by-service DevOps deployment guidelines for self-hosting.
* Integrating graphs and charts to visualize analytics within the application UI.
